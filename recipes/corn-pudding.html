Grandma's Corn Pudding

Descripton

Grandma's corn pudding is comfort food at its best.

This creamy corn casserole is easy to make with both whole kernel and cream-style corn, resulting in a custardy pudding that will be a welcome addition to your holiday table.

Ingredients

  • 5 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 2 (14.75 ounce) cans cream-style corn

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a 2-quart casserole dish.

  2. Whisk the eggs, then whisk in the milk, butter, sugar, and cornstarch.

  3. Stir in the drained canned whole kernel corn and cream-style corn.

  4. Pour mixture into the prepared casserole dish and bake until golden brown.

  5. Tuck in and Enjoy!
